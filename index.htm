About

Jaylen Ross

After her first child was born, Ms. Ross decided to try her hand at writing. "Though I enjoyed almost every minute of being a stay-at-home mom, I needed a creative outlet. I decided to write a book, just to see if I could actually do it. The first novel was a thrill to create and was an inspiration to family and friends. I loved pushing my mind to see what I could produce and apply; the twists and turns of a mystery, the burden of trials, the joy of redemption, and the many vicissitudes of romance and human emotion.

"Eventually, Olympic came along. Due to the nature of the story, as well as the inspiration that was ever-present in writing Olympic, I knew, without a doubt, that it needed to be published. I knew then as I know now, that it will change lives."

Olympic is a triumphant work that is hopeful and from the heart. With emphasis on faith and family, without being over-preachy, Ms. Ross gives readers a beautifully written ghost-story that is thought-provoking and tender.
